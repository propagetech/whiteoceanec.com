White Ocean Expert Consulting

In a VUCA (volatile, uncertain, complex and ambiguous) business environment, the ability to identify and acquire great leaders to lead companies is crucial. White Ocean Expert Consulting (WOEC) is a global retained executive search and consulting firm providing Executive Search and Consulting services. We specialize in identification and acquisition of leadership talent required in advisory or executive capacity by the clients globally. WOEC has the experienced consultants, assessment tools and techniques to help find and develop the right leaders, which can prove to be a valuable asset for any company.

Back to Bharat

Indian economy has recovered since the past few years and many foreign businesses are also looking to invest in India. In fact, in April 2016 India overtook China as the top destination for foreign investment [Source: The Indian Express ]. This has started a new trend; many NRIs are coming back to India. Indian manufacturers are also looking to expand abroad and obviously, the Indian industry needs professionals with international exposure as torch bearers to reach out to the world. We provide placements to NRIs wishing to return back to their motherland.

We strive to make our humble contribution by getting NRIs contribute to the nation’s growth along with the simultaneous realisation of their own professional goals. This trend of repatriation is not limited to the IT sector alone. Professionals from other sectors like banking, finance, pharma, automotive, clothing and food processing industries are also part of this trend.
